Based on a review of the provided transcript and relevant insights found
via a web search, here is a summary of the key points.

- **(00:33) Generative AI is a creative content creator.** Its primary
  function is to generate novel text, images, code, or music by learning
  patterns from vast datasets. It acts like a \"superpowered
  autocomplete\" but lacks understanding of real-world facts and can
  hallucinate. Tools like ChatGPT and Midjourney are examples.

- **(05:37) AI Agents are task*-o*riented assistants with tools.** They
  build upon generative AI by connecting the LLM \"brain\" to external
  tools, APIs, and short-term memory. This allows them to perform
  specific actions, like generating code in an IDE or booking a flight,
  rather than just creating content.

- **(08:24) Agentic AI is an autonomous orchestrator and planner.** This
  is the most advanced level, functioning as a system that manages
  multiple AI agents. It features a central planner (like a project
  manager), long-term memory, and feedback loops to execute complex,
  multi-step workflows (e.g., planning an entire vacation) with minimal
  human input.

- **The core difference is specialization vs. orchestration.** An AI
  Agent is a specialist that performs a single task well (e.g., a smart
  oven that sets its own temperature). Agentic AI is a general manager
  that coordinates multiple specialists and systems to achieve a larger
  goal (e.g., a smart kitchen hub that manages the oven, fridge, and
  pantry to suggest and prepare a meal).

- **Each type has a distinct model structure.** Generative AI is just an
  LLM. An AI Agent is an LLM plus tools/short-term memory. Agentic AI is
  an LLM plus a planner, multiple agents, tools, and long-term memory,
  enabling much higher autonomy.

- **Their real-world applications differ.** Generative AI is used for
  writing product descriptions (Beluk) or research summaries (Morgan
  Stanley). AI agents power customer support bots (Clara) or app
  automation (Zapier). Agentic AI manages e-commerce stores (Shopify
  Sidekick) or automates complex financial decisions.

- **Each category comes with unique limitations.** Generative AI can
  produce incorrect information. AI agents are limited to their
  predefined tasks and tools. Agentic AI, while powerful, requires
  strong guardrails to ensure it doesn\'t go off track when making
  autonomous decisions.
